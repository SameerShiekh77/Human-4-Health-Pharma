Product Description

CardioGuard Plus is a cutting-edge pharmaceutical solution specifically formulated to address cardiovascular health concerns. This advanced medication combines proven therapeutic agents to provide comprehensive support for patients managing hypertension and related cardiac conditions.

Developed through rigorous clinical research and testing, CardioGuard Plus has demonstrated exceptional efficacy in maintaining healthy blood pressure levels and promoting overall cardiovascular wellness. The medication works by targeting multiple pathways involved in blood pressure regulation, ensuring optimal therapeutic outcomes.

Key Benefits:

  • Effective blood pressure management and stabilization
  • Enhanced cardiovascular protection and support
  • Reduced risk of heart-related complications
  • Improved overall cardiac function and health
  • Long-lasting therapeutic effects with once-daily dosing
  • Well-tolerated with minimal side effects

CardioGuard Plus is manufactured in state-of-the-art facilities following strict quality control standards and Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP). Each batch undergoes comprehensive testing to ensure purity, potency, and safety.

Usage Instructions & Dosage

Recommended Dosage

Initial Dose: 10mg once daily, preferably in the morning
Maintenance Dose: 20mg once daily
Maximum Dose: 40mg once daily (as prescribed by physician)

How to Take

  • Take the tablet with a full glass of water
  • Can be taken with or without food
  • Swallow the tablet whole; do not crush, chew, or break
  • Take at the same time each day for best results
  • Continue taking as prescribed, even if you feel well

Important Note:

If you miss a dose, take it as soon as you remember. If it's almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose. Do not double the dose to catch up.

Possible Side Effects

Like all medications, CardioGuard Plus may cause side effects, although not everyone experiences them. Most side effects are mild and temporary.

Common Side Effects (Mild)

  • • Mild headache (usually temporary)
  • • Dizziness or lightheadedness
  • • Fatigue or tiredness
  • • Nausea or stomach discomfort

Less Common Side Effects

  • • Flushing or warmth sensation
  • • Swelling of ankles or feet
  • • Palpitations or irregular heartbeat
  • • Muscle cramps

Seek Immediate Medical Attention If You Experience:

  • ⚠️ Severe chest pain or tightness
  • ⚠️ Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • ⚠️ Signs of allergic reaction (rash, itching, swelling)
  • ⚠️ Severe dizziness or fainting

Precautions & Warnings

Before Taking CardioGuard Plus

Inform your healthcare provider if you:

  • Have any allergies to medications or ingredients
  • Are pregnant, planning to become pregnant, or breastfeeding
  • Have liver or kidney disease
  • Have diabetes or thyroid disorders
  • Are taking other medications (including over-the-counter and herbal supplements)

Special Warnings

Driving & Operating Machinery

This medication may cause dizziness. Avoid driving or operating heavy machinery until you know how this medicine affects you.

Alcohol Consumption

Limit or avoid alcohol as it may increase the risk of side effects and lower blood pressure excessively.

Pregnancy & Breastfeeding

Consult your doctor before use during pregnancy or while breastfeeding. This medication may not be suitable.

Drug Interactions

CardioGuard Plus may interact with:

  • • Other blood pressure medications
  • • Anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs)
  • • Certain antibiotics
  • • Diabetes medications
  • • Antidepressants

Always inform your healthcare provider about all medications you are taking.

Storage Instructions

Temperature

Store at room temperature between 20-25°C (68-77°F)

Light Protection

Keep in original packaging away from direct sunlight

Moisture

Store in a dry place; avoid moisture and humidity

Child Safety

Keep out of reach of children and pets

Important Storage Guidelines

  • • Do not use after the expiration date printed on the package
  • • Do not store in the bathroom or near sinks
  • • Do not freeze the medication
  • • Dispose of expired or unused medication properly

Disposal Information

Do not flush medications down the toilet or pour into drains. Consult your pharmacist or local waste disposal company for proper medication disposal methods.
