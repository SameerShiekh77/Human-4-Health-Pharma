Healthy Living Tips

Maintain a healthy BMI with these simple lifestyle changes

Regular Exercise

Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise daily

Quality Sleep

Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily

Important Note

BMI is a screening tool and should not be used as a diagnostic measure. It does not account for muscle mass, bone density, or distribution of fat. For a comprehensive health assessment, please consult with a healthcare professional.
